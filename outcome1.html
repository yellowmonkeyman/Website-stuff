1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business Data storage, manipulation and communication are a huge part of modern business practice. Information Technology has evolved to allow for a huge amount of data circulating through networks (including the internet) and more data storage than ever. The user base of computers has increased alongside this, partly due to the accessibility of computer systems. Business are therefore finding that they can use IT to automate certain processes, access the now massive user base of the internet and store data collected from these interactions and processes.
